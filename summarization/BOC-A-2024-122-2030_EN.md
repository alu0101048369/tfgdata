Text:
In accordance with the provisions of articles 17.3.b) and 20.8.a) of Law 38/2003, of November 17, General Subsidies, the extract of the call is published, the full text of which can be consulted in the Database National Subsidies (https://www.infosubvenciones.es/bdnstrans/GE/es/convocatoria/768217).
First.- Call.
The Awards are announced for the best Final Degree Projects, Final Master's Projects for the 2023-2024 academic year of the “Chair of Childhood and Adolescence of the University of La Laguna”.
Second.- Purpose.
The objective of these awards is the promotion of training, research and innovation activity among the students of the ULL and the ULPGC in the topics that are the objective of the Chair, as well as the dissemination of the results of the Work already carried out. and the stimulation to carry out future academic work that contributes to promoting knowledge and research on topics that are beneficial for childhood and adolescence.
Third.- Beneficiaries.
Students from the University of La Laguna or the University of Las Palmas de Gran Canaria who meet the following requirements can obtain one of the awards:
• Have completed the TFG/TFM at the University of La Laguna or the University of Las Palmas de Gran Canaria.
• That the Works are related to the theme of the call.
• Have defended and passed the TFG/TFM in the 2023-2024 academic year.
• Have obtained a minimum grade of at least 8.5 out of 10.
The call for these awards will be resolved before the budget closing of the 2024 financial year.
Fourth.- Awards.
The following prizes will be awarded:
Three prizes for TFG: a first prize of 1,000 euros, a second of 600 euros and a third of 400 euros.
Three prizes for TFM: a first prize of 1,000 euros, a second prize of 600 euros and a third prize of 400 euros.
Assigned budget keys: 1804022317 455A2 48701 Chapter IV for an amount of 4,000 euros.
All winners will receive the corresponding accreditation diploma.
The prizes will be subject to personal income tax withholding as established by current legislation.
Fifth.- Form of presentation of applications.
The place of presentation will be through the electronic headquarters of the University of La Laguna https://sede.ull.es/ecivilis-site/catalog/showProcedure/229, providing the required documentation, in addition to those provided for in article 16.4 of the Law 39/2015, of October 1, of the Common Administrative Procedure of Public Administrations.
Through the following link you can consult information about the documentation to be presented and any other information of interest about the call: https://www.ull.es/portal/convocatorias/categorias/premios/
The form that must accompany the General Application will be available on the institutional website of the University of La Laguna at the following address https://www.ull.es/portal/convocatorias/categorias/otras-convocatorias/
Sixth.- Deadline for submitting applications.
The deadline to submit the documentation to participate in this call will begin the day following its publication in the Official Gazette of the Canary Islands and will end on July 31, 2024.
San Cristóbal de La Laguna, June 13, 2024.- The Vice-Rector of Culture and University Extension (p.d. Resolution of 12.5.2023; BOC no. 101, of 25.5.2023), Isabel Karely León Pérez.


Summary:
The General Subsidies call aims to promote training, research and innovation activity among the students of the University of La Laguna and the ULPGC in the topics that are the objective of the Chair of Childhood and Adolescence. The call is open to students who have completed the TFG/TFM at the University of La Laguna or the University of Las Palmas de Gran Canaria and have defended and passed the TFG/TFM in the 2023-2024 academic year. The winners will receive the corresponding accreditation diploma and prizes assigned based on the type of work submitted. The deadline for submitting applications is July 31, 2024.

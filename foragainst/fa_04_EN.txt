Instructions:
Give arguments for and against the given topic

Given topic:
Increase defense spending


Arguments:
1. Increased defense spending will strengthen the U.S. military and deter potential adversaries.
2. It will enhance national security and protect the interests of the United States.
3. It will create jobs and boost the economy.
4. It will improve the quality of life for veterans and their families.

Counterarguments:
1. It could lead to an arms race and increase tensions between countries.
2. It could increase the risk of military intervention and unintended consequences.
3. It could divert resources away from other important government programs.
4. It could lead to higher taxes and reduced government services.
